Our Services

Vicnic Water Pump Repair offers a comprehensive range of services for residential, agricultural, and commercial clients throughout Zimbabwe. Our skilled technicians deliver fast, reliable, and affordable solutions tailored to your water system needs.

  • Water Pump Repairs: Diagnosis and repair of all major pump brands and models.
  • Routine Maintenance: Preventative maintenance to keep your pumps running efficiently and extend their lifespan.
  • Installation & Replacement: Professional installation or replacement of new and existing water pumps.
  • Borehole Pump Services: Borehole pump installation, repair, and recovery.
  • Water Tank & Reticulation: Setup and maintenance of water tanks and reticulation systems.
  • Solar Water Pump Solutions: Installation and maintenance of energy-saving solar pumping systems.
  • Emergency Call-Outs: Rapid response for urgent water supply breakdowns.
  • Consultation & System Upgrades: Expert advice and upgrades to improve water delivery and efficiency.
